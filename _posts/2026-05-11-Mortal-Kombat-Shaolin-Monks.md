<p><b>Story</b></p>
<p>The story follows two earthrealm warriors: Liu Kang and Kung Lao, aided by Raiden the God of thunder, our warriors fight to stop Shang Tsung from draging eartrealm into an unlawful Mortal Kombat tournament, in which if earthrealm looses. The forces of dark will conquere it. To stop this from happening, our fighters travel to the darkest corner of the world : Outworld.  </p>
<p><B>Game Mechanics</B></p>
<p>
  What I love about this game it's all the mechanics in it, it's an rpg style where you can play with your friend/sibling the story mode in the same screen ( We have the same feature in Call of Duty Black Ops 1,2 in Zombies: Split Screen) it's called ko-up. You can gather points to buy combos and special moves, there is puzzle solving too and many other cool mechanics I wish modern games had.
</p>
