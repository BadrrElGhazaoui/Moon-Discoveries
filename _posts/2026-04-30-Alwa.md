<p>When the French forces entered Morocco during the periode of the French "Protectorate". It divided Morocco to two parts, Useful Morocco
and Useless Morocco. The region of Chaouia found itself in Useful Morocco due to it's resourceful hills, people of that region used to
used to call hills, alwa or khalwa, which means a high place. During this periode is where a new music genre that would
change the game will be born : Al Alwa-العلوة</p>
