<p><b>Story</b></p>
<p>
  Najat Aatabou, one of the many pillars that held/still hold Moroccan music/tradition on their backs.<br>
  The story behind this song is that in her early teens, Najat used to sneak from her house to sing in local weddings, to make money and for the love of the art. <br>
  One day in one of those weddings, someone filmed her while singing this song "J'en ai marre", it blew up. Everyone began selling her recording in the black market.<br>
  Years later the first ever recording was born, in 1987. Here it is:
