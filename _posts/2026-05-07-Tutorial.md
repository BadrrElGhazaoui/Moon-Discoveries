<p><b>Recap</b></p>
<p>Let's put the steps in order and explain each step.
1- Install Multiman 
2- Install Ps2 Iso from a Ps2 Roms Website,<b><a href="https://romsfun.com/roms/playstation-2/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" style="color: black; text-decoration: none;">example</a></b>
3- Use Multiman to Encrypt Ps2 Iso . Go to the last section on Multiman , switch multiman to CFW . Go back and click on the ps2 Iso It will say if you want to encrypt click yes
4- After encrypting the Iso . Go to Webman, go to the ps2 games section and click on the game you want to play.
5- After choosing your game. Webman will show you Ps2 placeholder click on it and Enjoy! </p>
<p><b>Bonus</b></p>
<p>If you want to dowload games on your ps3, there are 3 ways . The Classical way is to use a fat32 usb and plug it to Multiman . The Problem here is the game can't be more than 4GB
. The Second way is format your usb to NTFS, and use Multiman to show it's files after switching to CFW mode and cliking NTFS scan. The 3rd way and my fav is use ftp.
For linux just go to files and type ftp://your ip adress here . And you will access the ps3 files and move your games in PS3ISO/PS2ISO/PS1ISO.It will take time but it gives you full control and it's easy.
For Windows dowload an ftp client like FileZilla.</p>
